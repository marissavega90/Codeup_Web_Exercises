Zion National Park

  • Zion is a Hebrew word that means ‘a place of peace and relaxation.'
  • Approximately 2.5 million people visit the park each year.
  • The highest elevation in the park is at Kolob Canyon at 8,726 feet
  • The California Condor, a bird considered one of the most endangered species, can be found in the park.

The Grand Canyon

  • The Grand Canyon was one of our very first national parks.
  • There aren’t any dinosaur fossils at the Grand Canyon
  • The Grand Canyon is the second largest canyon in the world
  • TThe widest point of the Grand Canyon is farther than the average American’s commute to work.
